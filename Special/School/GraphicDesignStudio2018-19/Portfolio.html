My name is Kian M, and this is a compilation of what I believe to be my best work in Graphic Design Studio.


Semester 1


My semester favorite
Type Specimen Poster

The objective of this project was to create a quasi ad for a typeface and display it in as many variations as possible, be it variable weight or emphasis.

I'm very straightforward when it comes to my hobby. I like to program, so I chose a popular monospace typeface used in my primary code editor.

Completed on Oct. 17, 2018
Victorian Era Poster

The objective of this project was to research a particular era of Graphic Design, and produce a poster which accurately represents the formatting of posters from the era.

This one was a bit of a challenge, as the more dated a concept is, the more I typiaclly despise it soley for not being modern. Luckilly, Samuel Brown's invention of the Gas Vacuum engine fit right in the 1819-1901 time slot.

Completed on Nov. 13, 2018
1 Hour Halloween Poster

The objective of this project was to create a movie poster in 1 hour with found imagery.

The movie I decided to make a poster for was Carrie. I honestly wouldn't be able to tell you why, as I haven't seen the movie.

Completed on Oct. 10, 2018
Color Theory

The objective of this project was to familiarize myself with the Adobe Color Wheel and color schemes such as Monochromatic, Complementary, and Analogous colors.

The first thing that popped into my head was a Plex-esqe icon, and I colored the four sections from there. I picked the Monochromatic, Complementary, and Double Complementary color schemes.

Completed on Dec. 12, 2018

Semester 2


My semester favorite
Travel Postcard

The objective of this project was to create a postcard for a setting in a book I had read in school.

I chose The Hatchet, mostly because it was one of the only books I remembered both reading and watching the movie for. This is the only project in my GDS 2018-19 portfolio with 3D rendered artwork.

Completed on Feb. 22, 2019
Buttons

The objective of this project was to design 12 all original related buttons for the the school's Fine Arts Week.

I centered around the admittedly lazy prospect of making most of my buttons the file format icons across Adobe products, and a few ideas I had while making them.

Completed on Mar. 11, 2019
Staff Birthday Card

The objective of this project was to design a birthday card for staff members at our high school.

I chose to make something of a corporate card (A corporation would never make something like this) instead of a bad birthday pun.

Completed on Mar. 20, 2019

Album Cover

The objective of this project was to design an album cover that conveys the mood of the underlying music.

I enjoyed Hanz Zimmer's theme for the Interstellar movie, so I designed a cover based on it's soft sounds mixed with a space theme from the movie it accompanied.

Completed on Apr. 22, 2019
Social Issue Pamphlet

This pamphlet is 1 part of a multi-person project. The objective was to create a pamphlet for our group's decided social issue, lack of vaccination and the risk stemming from it.

I chose a very light color scheme to make the pamphlet seem as friendly as possible for readers who may not fully trust it.

Completed on May 10, 2019
Zine

The objective of the project was to create a Maga(Zine) on a particular topic. I decided to make mine about building a PC.

This project was supposed to be 16 pages, but I only finished 7. I don't even get to the actual buying or building part of building a PC in the text I've provided. All I give is a broad overview of the parts and their significance in particular workloads.

Completed on June 3, 2019
